> ## âš–ï¸ Load Theory of Attention and Cognitive Control: Reconciling Selection Mechanisms
> - **Scope & Angle:** This topic explores Lavie's Load Theory, which resolves the early versus late selection debate by proposing that perceptual load determines the locus of attentional selection.
