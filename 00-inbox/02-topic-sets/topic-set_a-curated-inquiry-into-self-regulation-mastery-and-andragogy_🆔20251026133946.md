### Further Exploration

To deepen your mastery, you may find these related subtopics valuable for further investigation:

  - **Metacognition & Cognitive Reappraisal:** The specific "thinking about thinking" skills and the technique of changing emotional responses by reframing the meaning of a situation.
  - **Self-Determination Theory (SDT):** A core motivational theory focusing on **Autonomy, Competence, and Relatedness** as fundamental psychological needs that drive growth and behavior.
  - **Implementation Intentions:** The "if-then" planning strategy (e.g., "If I feel X, then I will do Y") that is a cornerstone of effective behavioral implementation.
  - **Bandura's Theory of Self-Efficacy:** Explores the critical role of one's *belief* in their ability to succeed as a primary determinant of in successfully managing their behavior.
  - **Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT):** A therapeutic model that focuses on psychological flexibility, mindfulness, and value-based action, rather than directly fighting negative thoughts.
