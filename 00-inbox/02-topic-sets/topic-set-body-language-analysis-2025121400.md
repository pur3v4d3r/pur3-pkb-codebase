> - **Scope & Angle:** This topic explores the systematic process of establishing individual behavioral baselines and detecting meaningful deviations that signal emotional state changes, stress, or deception. Rather than relying on stereotypical "tells," expert analysts first observe how a particular individual behaves under normal, comfortable conditions, then identify clusters of changes from that norm. The focus is on the methodological rigor required to avoid confirmation bias and false positivesâ€”understanding that a crossed arm might mean defensiveness for one person but simply comfort for another.
