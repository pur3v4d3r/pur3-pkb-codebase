# Further Exploration

To deepen your mastery of Dataview, you may find the following related subtopics valuable for further investigation.

- **Styling Dataview Outputs:** An exploration of using CSS snippets to customize the appearance of your Dataview tables and lists to better integrate them into your notes and dashboards.
- **Performance and Optimization:** A look into how Dataview indexes your vault and the best practices for writing efficient queries to ensure your vault remains fast as it grows.
- **The `dataview-api`:** An investigation into the plugin's underlying API, which is used by DataviewJS and other plugins (like Dataview-Charts) to access the Dataview index.
- **Dataview `Views`:** A study of the (newer) "Views" feature, which allows you to define reusable query components using JavaScript and markdown, promoting a more modular approach to dashboard building.
