Title: Topics: The Art and Science of Daily Personal Knowledge Base Planning

> [!the-purpose]
> This topic set explores the methodologies, cognitive principles, and systemic structures behind effective daily planning for a Personal Knowledge Base (Personal Knowledge Base). Moving beyond simple "to-do lists," these topics frame daily planning as a high-level strategic activity. They cover the integration of formal methodologies like GTD and Zettelkasten into a daily workflow, the role of cognitive science in managing attention and preventing overload, and the behavioral psychology of building sustainable habits. Furthermore, the topics investigate how the very architecture of a Personal Knowledge Base system can be designed to reduce friction and how the "daily note" artifact can be elevated from a mere log to a central cognitive dashboard.
