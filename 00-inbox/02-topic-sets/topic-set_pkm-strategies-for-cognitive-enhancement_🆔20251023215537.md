Title: `PKM Strategies for Cognitive Enhancement`

> [!the-purpose]
> This topic set explores the intersection of Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) with advanced cognitive skills. It moves beyond simple note-taking to frame PKM as an active "thinking environment." The topics cover how PKM systems can be engineered to serve as decision-support tools, frameworks for analytical deconstruction, and practical instruments for metacognitive reflection. It also investigates how applying formal pedagogical theories to your PKM design can transform it into a powerful, self-directed learning engine.
