Aliases: [[CogSci-Based PKM Design]], [[The Psychology of Knowledge Work]], [[Neuro-aligned Information Systems]]

> [!the-purpose]
> This topic set bridges the gap between abstract cognitive science principles and the practical architecture of Personal Knowledge Bases (PKB). It explores how the brain encodes, retrieves, and synthesizes information and translates those biological mechanisms into digital workflows. The topics cover the optimization of cognitive load, the application of Self-Determination Theory to maintenance habits, the role of Constructivism in note-linking, and the use of Self-Regulated Learning cycles for incremental mastery.
