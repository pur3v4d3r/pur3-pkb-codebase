# The "SOCRATIC" Framework for Interactive Inquiry

This is less of a single prompt and more of a method for a conversational deep-dive. It uses questioning to unpack a topic layer by layer, which is perfect for truly understanding the "why" behind an idea. You start with a broad question and use the AI's answer to form your next, more specific question.

- **1. Foundational Question:** Start with a broad, open-ended question. ("What is justice?")
    
- **2. Clarifying Questions:** Ask for definitions of key terms used in the answer. ("You mentioned 'distributive justice.' What exactly does that mean?")
    
- **3. Probing Assumptions:** Challenge the underlying assumptions in the AI's explanation. ("Why is fairness considered the most critical component of justice?")
    
- **4. Exploring Implications:** Ask about the logical consequences of the idea. ("If that's true, what are the implications for our modern legal system?")
    
- **5. Seeking Counter-Perspectives:** Ask for alternative views. ("How would a utilitarian philosopher respond to that definition of justice?")
    

**Example of a Conversational Flow:**

 **You:** "What is the core idea behind quantum entanglement?" **AI:** "It's a phenomenon where two or more particles become linked in such a way that their fates are intertwined, no matter the distance separating themâ€¦" **You:** "What do you mean by their 'fates are intertwined'?" **AI:** "It means that if you measure a property of one particle, like its spin, you instantly know the corresponding property of the other particleâ€¦" **You:** "Why is it significant that this happens 'instantly,' regardless of distance?"
