```component
# The "A-to-Z" Framework for Comprehensive Exploration

When you need a deep, holistic understanding of a topic from all angles, this framework is your guide. It ensures you cover everything from historical context to future implications.

- **A - Antecedent:** What is the history, origin, or background of this topic?
    
- **C - Core Principles:** What are the fundamental concepts, theories, or defining principles?
    
- **E - Examples:** Provide concrete examples, case studies, or real-world applications.
    
- **I - Implications:** What are the broader impacts, consequences, or significance of this topic?
    
- **O - Objections:** What are the main criticisms, controversies, or alternative viewpoints?
    
- **U - Future (You):** Where is this topic heading? What are the future trends, predictions, or unresolved questions?
    

**Example Prompt:**

 "Provide a comprehensive overview of the philosophy of Stoicism using the A-to-Z framework. **(A)ntecedent:** Cover its origins in ancient Greece. **(C)ore Principles:** Explain key ideas like the dichotomy of control, virtue, and living in accordance with nature. **(E)xamples:** Discuss historical figures like Marcus Aurelius and Seneca. **(I)mplications:** Analyze its impact on modern psychology, like CBT. **(O)bjections:** Address common criticisms, such as it promoting passionlessness. **(U)ltimate Future:** Discuss its relevance and future in the modern world."

```
