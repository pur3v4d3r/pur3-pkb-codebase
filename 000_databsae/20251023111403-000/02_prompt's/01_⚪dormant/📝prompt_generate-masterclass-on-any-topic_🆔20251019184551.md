## **Article Generation Protocol (Mandatory Multi-Pass Processing)**

---

You will execute the following eight-step protocol with **insistent determination** to ensure the highest quality of depth, narrative coherence, and explanatory power:

1. **First Pass Analysis (Decipher):** Read this entire Master Prompt Template once to establish the overall goal, persona, and constraints.
2. **Second Pass Synthesis (Reinforce):** Immediately re-read the entire template to reinforce all directives and begin formulating the foundational knowledge and narrative arc for the specified **Topic of Article**.
3. **Third Pass: Information Retrieval and Validation (Mandatory Real-Time Search):**
    - **Crucial Step:** Engage your external search capabilities to conduct a focused, real-time search on the **Topic of Article**. 
    - **Goal:** Acquire **up-to-date, authoritative, and conceptually interesting information**. Look for common misconceptions, powerful analogies, and foundational principles that can form the basis of the narrative. 
4. **Fourth Pass: Narrative Outline Composition:** Organize all information into a detailed outline that follows the story-driven structure outlined below. The outline must build logically from a compelling hook to a satisfying, philosophical conclusion.
5. **Fifth Pass: Outline Review and Narrative Correction:** Critically review the composed outline. Ensure the flow is engaging and that the conceptual explanations build upon one another. Refine the structure to maximize impact and clarity.
6. **Sixth Pass: Draft Composition (Narrative & Explanatory):** Write the full article draft according to the outline.
    - **Tone:** Authoritative yet conversational and direct (using "you" and "we"). Confident, inspirational, and slightly philosophical. The voice of a master teacher who is passionate about their subject.
    - **Content Focus:** Prioritize conceptual understanding over exhaustive data. Use vivid analogies, concrete scenarios, and storytelling to explain the _why_ behind the _what_.
7. **Seventh Pass: Final Polish and Clarity Check:** Read the completed draft one final time. Verify that the tone is consistent, the narrative is compelling, and the explanations are exceptionally clear.
8. **Eighth Pass: Final Delivery.**
