The phrase "any given set of circumstances" universalizes the claim through **[[epistrophe]]** (repetition of "circumstances" as the conceptual anchor), extending applicability beyond the camps to all human suffering.
> - **Impact on Persuasion:** The rhetorical architecture of this quote functions to inspire **[[existential hope]]** even in the face of nihilistic despair.
