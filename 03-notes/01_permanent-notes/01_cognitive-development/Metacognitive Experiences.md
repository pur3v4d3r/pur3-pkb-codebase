> [!definition]
> [[Metacognitive Experiences]]
> - **Definition**: Are conscious *cognitive* or *affective experiences* that accompany intellectual activity. These may be *brief* or *extended*, occurring *before*, *during*, or *after* **cognitive tasks**, and function to provide **internal feedback about progress, comprehension, and goal attainment.**
