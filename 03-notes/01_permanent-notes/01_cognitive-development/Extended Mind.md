# Extended Mind

> [!definition]
> - **Key-Term**:[[Extended Mind]]
> - [**Definition**:This is a philosophical and cognitive science theory proposing that the human mind is not solely contained within the brain and skull, but extends into the environment and incorporates external tools and resources (such as notebooks or smartphones) as functional parts of the cognitive system.]
