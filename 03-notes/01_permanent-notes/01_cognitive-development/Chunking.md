> [!definition]
> - **Key-Term**: [[Chunking]]
> - [**Definition**: The process by which individual elements can be recoded into larger, more meaningful units that each occupy only a single "slot" in working memory.]

[[Working Memory]] consists of activated portions of [[Long-Term Memory]]. Which in turn is nestled within the [[Focus Of Attention]]. All of which can hold only *4* [[Chunking|chunks]] of information.
Then when an individual has sufficient time and resources to engage in [[Chunking]] then you have the *7 plus or minus 2*
