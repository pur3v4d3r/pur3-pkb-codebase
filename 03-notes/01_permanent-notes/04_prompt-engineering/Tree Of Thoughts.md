> - [**Definition**:: [[Tree-of-Thoughts]] ([[Tree-of-Thoughts|TOT]]) is a conceptual framework in artificial intelligence that extends the idea of Chain of Thought prompting by exploring multiple reasoning paths, similar to a tree search, allowing large language models to evaluate different intermediate steps and backtrack or commit to the most promising sequence of thoughts to solve a complex problem.]
