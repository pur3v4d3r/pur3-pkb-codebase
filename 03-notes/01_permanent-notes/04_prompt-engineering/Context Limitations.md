> [!definition]
> - **Key-Term**:: [[Context Limitations]]
> - [**Definition**:: The model loses track of earlier information or fails to integrate information across long contexts.]

 - **Solution**: Compress information **strategically**, use *summaries* of **earlier context**, employ **multi-turn approaches** where each turn *processes manageable chunks*, position **critical information** *prominently*.
