> [!definition]
> - **Key-Term**:: [[Reasoning Failures]]
> - [**Definition**:: The model employs flawed logic or makes unjustified inferential leaps.]

 - **Solution**: Use [[Chain-of-Thought]] prompting to make reasoning **explicit** and **checkable**, *provide examples* of correct reasoning patterns, *break* *complex reasoning* into simpler steps, employ [[Self-Consistency]] to filter out spurious reasoning paths.
