> [!definition]
> - **Key-Term**:: [[Semantic Precision]]
> - [**Definition**:: Semantic precision refers to the degree to which a retrieved set of items, such as documents or search results, are relevant to the intended meaning or conceptual scope of the user's query or information need, focusing on the accuracy of the underlying concepts rather than just keyword matching.]
