> [!definition]
> - **Key-Term**:: [[Gemini]]
> - [**Definition**:: Is a family of multimodal large language models (LLMs) from Google DeepMind. It is designed to understand and process different types of information at once. These include text, images, audio, video, and computer code.]

> [!important] Key Features of Gemini
> - **Multimodality**: [[Gemini]] was built to be multimodal. This means users can provide mixed inputs and receive integrated, contextually relevant outputs.
> - **Transformer Architecture**: The models use the transformer architecture. This deep learning model uses a self-attention mechanism.
> - **Scalability**: The Gemini family includes different model sizes. Each is optimized for specific tasks and platforms.
> - **Integration**: [[Gemini]] is the underlying AI model that powers Google's generative AI chatbot, also named [[Gemini]], formerly Bard. It is integrated across Google products and services, including Search, Gmail, Docs, and Android devices.
> - **Agentic Capabilities**: The latest versions can generate content, plan, reason, and interact with external tools and APIs to complete multi-step tasks. 
> 
> 
> [[Gemini]] is a versatile and powerful AI engine designed to be a highly capable personal and professional assistant across the Google ecosystem.
