> [!definition]
> - **Key-Term**:: [[In-Context Learning]]
> - [**Definition**:: In-Context Learning is a capability of large language models (LLMs) to learn new tasks or adapt their behavior based on examples provided directly within the input prompt, without requiring explicit weight updates or fine-tuning of the model.]
