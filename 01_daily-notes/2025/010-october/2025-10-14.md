### Completed Tasks from Yesterday

```Dataview
TASK
WHERE completed
WHERE completion = (date(today) - dur(1 day))
GROUP BY file.link
```

```Dataview
TASK
WHERE !completed
WHERE file.cday = (date(today) - dur(1 day))
GROUP BY file.link
```
