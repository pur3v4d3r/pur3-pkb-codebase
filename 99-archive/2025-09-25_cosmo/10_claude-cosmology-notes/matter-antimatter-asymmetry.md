## üî¨ The Sakharov Conditions

### Andrei Sakharov (1967): Three Requirements
For matter-antimatter asymmetry to develop dynamically:

#### 1. **Baryon Number Violation**
- **Definition**: Processes that change total number of baryons
- **Need**: To go from zero net baryon number to excess
- **GUTs**: Grand Unified Theories predict proton decay
- **Status**: Required, predicted by theory, not yet observed

#### 2. **C and CP Violation**  
- **C Violation**: Charge conjugation symmetry breaking
- **CP Violation**: Combined charge-parity symmetry breaking
- **Need**: Distinguish matter from antimatter
- **Observation**: CP violation discovered in K-mesons, B-mesons

#### 3. **Departure from Thermal Equilibrium**
- **Need**: Allow asymmetry to develop and persist
- **Equilibrium**: Would wash out any developing asymmetry
- **Mechanisms**: Phase transitions, particle decays, expansion
- **Cosmic Setting**: Expanding universe naturally out of equilibrium
