## ðŸŽ­ Extra Dimensional Theories

### Braneworld Models
- **Concept**: We live on 4D "brane" in higher-dimensional "bulk"
- **Gravity**: Leaks into extra dimensions on large scales
- **Effect**: Modifies gravity law at cosmological distances
- **Kaluza-Klein**: Higher-dimensional gravity appears as forces

#### DGP Model (Dvali-Gabadadze-Porrati)
- **Setup**: 4D brane in 5D bulk space
- **Crossover**: Gravity transitions from 4D to 5D behavior
- **Acceleration**: Natural cosmic acceleration without dark energy
- **Problems**: Ghosts, superluminal gravitons, ruled out by data

### String Theory Modifications
- **Dilaton**: Additional scalar field from string theory
- **Gauss-Bonnet**: Higher-order curvature terms
- **Warped Dimensions**: Non-factorizable extra dimensions
- **Status**: Highly speculative, limited concrete predictions

### Verlinde's Emergent Gravity (2016)
- **Concept**: Gravity emerges from thermodynamics of information
- **Dark Matter**: Apparent dark matter from emergent gravitational effects
- **Prediction**: Specific relation between baryonic matter and "apparent" dark matter
- **Status**: Some successes with galaxy rotation curves, but challenges with clusters

### Thermodynamic Approaches
- **Holographic**: Gravity from holographic principle
- **Entropy**: Gravitational dynamics from entropy considerations
- **Information**: Spacetime geometry encodes information
- **Speculation**: Highly theoretical, limited testable predictions
