**Record where you’ve searched:**
> [!INFO] Part of a thorough workflow is noting which search engines, databases, and terms you’ve already tried. This prevents redundancy and shows where you might need to search next.

> [!NOTE] For academic thoroughness (especially in literature reviews), it’s good practice to document your search process (e.g., “Searched Google Scholar on Sept 19, 2025 for ‘renewable energy adoption barriers’ – 200 results reviewed”).

> [!TLDR] Such a log ensures you cover all bases and can even be appended to a report to demonstrate the rigor of your research.
