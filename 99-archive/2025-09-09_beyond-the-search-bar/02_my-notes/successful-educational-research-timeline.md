> [!TLDR]
> The universal progression framework applies across all specialized domains.

> [!INFO] Novice Researchers (months 1-3)
> Novice researchers focus on understanding basic terminology, identifying key resources, joining professional associations, and following leading experts.

> [!INFO] Advanced Beginners (months 4-9)
> Advanced beginners read review papers, attend virtual conferences, participate in forums, and network with other learners.

> [!INFO] Competent Practitioners (months 10-18)
> Competent practitioners understand research frontiers, engage with primary literature, build expert relationships, and contribute to community discussions.

> [!INFO] Proficient Researchers (months 19-36)
> Proficient researchers conduct original analysis, present findings, mentor beginners, and collaborate on projects.
